Built for you, by us: our story

Kantoka is a story about growth, discovery, and personal journeys. The platform was founded by a group that came together while exploring the space individually in search of solutions that closed the gap between consumers and products in the legal cannabis space. Putting the pieces together, we realized there needed to be something vastly different in the marketplace, something that catered to all walks of life in a meaningful way, something the streamlined the consumer's journey.

Kantoka gives the new age consumer a trusted and reliable platform to learn and explore the ever evolving cannabis industry. Our journey of learning and self- education has become the basis for building a platform that educates the modern consumer.

Our roots are West Coast, but our aspirations are global. With cannabis legalization happening across theworld, Kantoka is dedicated to provide consumers with products that they deserve and expect.

Our passion is to bring the highest quality products to the end consumer.Our vision is for our customers to evolve and grow with the plant.

That's our story. Authentic. Unfiltered. Something different.
